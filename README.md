Given:

$$\frac{-4x}{3} - 8 = -2 - \frac{10x}{7}$$

Transfer $-\frac{10x}{7}$ to left side:

$$\frac{-4x}{3} + \frac{10x}{7} - 8 = -2$$

Transfer $-8$ to right side and get $8-2=6$:

$$\frac{-4x}{3} + \frac{10x}{7} = 6$$

Butterfly method:

$$\frac{-28x + 30x}{21} = 6$$

Subtract $30x-28x=2x$:

$$\frac{2x}{21} = 6$$

Transfer $21$ to right side.

$$2x = 6 \times 21$$

Multiply $6$ and $21$ to get $126$, then transfer $2$ (from $2x$) to right side.

$$x = \frac{126}{2}$$

Divide $\frac{126}{2}$ and get answer:

$$\boxed{x = 63$}$
